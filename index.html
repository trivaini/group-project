HOMEOSTASIS

Homeostasis is the maintenance of the internal environment of the body. Homeostasis is maintained despite considerable stress or changes to the body: anxiety, exercise, age, puberty, pregnancy, temperature, altutide etc.

  • Anxiety
  • Exercise
  • Age
  • Puberty
  • Pregnancy
  • Temperature
  • Altitude

Homeo= sameness Stasis = state

Types of Homeostatic Regulation

  1. Thermoregulation
  2. Osmoregulation
  3. Chemical Regulation
